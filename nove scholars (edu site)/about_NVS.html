About Nova Scholars

We build confident learners who can study, practice, and improve every day.

Nova Scholars is a simple learning hub built around the subjects students actually need. The goal is to make revision easier, CBT practice familiar, and study resources easy to reach from any device.

Join the platform Browse resources

Mission

Help students prepare for exams with focused resources, fast practice, and a learning flow that keeps them consistent.

Vision

Become a trusted study companion for learners who want a cleaner path to better results.

What makes it different

  • Simple navigation across every study page.
  • Clear study tracks for science, English, and CBT.
  • Resources designed for quick revision and stronger recall.
